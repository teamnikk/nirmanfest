About the Fest

Where Engineers
Build Legends

ESTRUCTURA 2K26 is the flagship annual fest of the Civil Engineering Department at DCRUST Murthal — organized by Nirmaan Society. It's a day where textbook knowledge collides with real-world engineering instincts.

Compete in bridge-building, quiz battles, quantity surveying, structural Jenga, and more. All participants receive certificates of participation. Winners take home cash prizes and engineering glory.
